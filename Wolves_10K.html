10th Annual Running with the Wolves 10K
New This Year - White Fang 5k Option!

Race Details

WHEN: Wednesday, July 18, 2018, at 6:30pm
WHERE: Kelleher Field, 72 Jefferson St., Marlboro, MA. Same start and finish line for both races.
BIB PICKUP: Tuesday, July 17, 2018 at Medusa Brewing Company, 11 Main Street, Hudson, MA from 4pm - 7pm and at the race on race day starting at 5pm.
WHAT:
  • USATF certified 10k race and NEW 5k race option
  • Out & back course for both races on the car-free Assabet River Rail Trail
  • RUNNERS ONLY IN 10K RACE. Walkers and runners may enter the 5k.
  • Technical race shirts and other SWAG to all registered participants
  • Medals to first place male and female finishers, and fastest male and female finishers in each age group: Under 18, 18-29, 30-39, 40-49, 50-59 and 60+
  • Special trophy to the team/corporation with the most registered participants
  • Chip timing presented by RaceWire
  • A 90-minute time limit will apply to the 10k. Runners who have not reached the turnaround point after 45 minutes will be turned around on the course by volunteers.
  • Post-race party with live music, food and drinks at the Prospector East Saloon, 520 Lincoln Street, Marlborough

PRESENTED BY THE HIGHLAND CITY STRIDERS RUNNING CLUB
Highland City Striders (HCS) Running Club is a group of adult runners who live in the greater Marlboro-Hudson area, who run for fun and to keep in shape since 2004. HCS also organizes non-profit races to raise money for charity and to promote the sport of running.

The Highland City Striders is a non-profit 501(C)3. All race profits will go to local charities. Since 2009, the Running with the Wolves 10k has raised over $50,000 for charity.
